Privacy Policy for Blyss Dating App

Last updated: December 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Blyss ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile dating application.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name, email address, age, gender, and birthdate
  • Profile photos and bio
  • Location data (approximate and precise)
  • Messages and chat content
  • Device information and identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our dating service
  • To match you with other users based on location and preferences
  • To enable communication between matched users
  • To verify age (18+ requirement)
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To send notifications about matches and messages
  • To prevent fraud and ensure safety

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We share your information with:

  • Other Users: Your profile information is visible to other authenticated users for matching purposes
  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google), AdMob for advertisements
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics for app improvement
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore: User data storage and authentication
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Push notifications
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reporting and app stability
  • Google AdMob: Advertising (see AdMob privacy policy)
  • Google Maps: Location services
  • Google Play In-App Billing: Processing subscription purchases

6. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Blyss offers optional premium subscriptions that unlock additional features including:

  • See who liked you
  • Ad-free experience
  • Unlimited likes
  • Priority matching
  • Profile boost
  • Undo swipes

Subscriptions are processed through Google Play In-App Billing. Your subscription status is stored in our database to enable premium features. You can cancel subscriptions at any time through your Google Play account settings. Refunds are subject to Google Play's refund policy.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time through the app settings, which will permanently remove your data from our systems within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of location sharing
  • Block or report other users
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request a copy of your data (GDPR/CCPA)

9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication
  • Firebase security rules to protect data access
  • Regular security audits

10. Age Restriction

Blyss is strictly for users 18 years and older. We verify age during signup and do not knowingly collect information from minors.

11. Location Data

We collect location data to match you with nearby users. You can control location permissions through your device settings. Disabling location may limit app functionality.

12. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and advertising purposes through Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics.

13. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in servers located in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

14. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights including the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt-out of data sales. We do not sell personal information.

15. GDPR Compliance

For EU users, we comply with GDPR requirements including data portability, right to erasure, and consent management.

16. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

17. Contact Us

For privacy concerns or data requests, contact us at:

Email: privacy@blyssapp.com

18. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the app through Settings > Account > Delete Account. Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing us at:

Email: delete@blyssapp.com

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will permanently remove all your personal data from our systems within 30 days.
